# Customer Personas
## Persona 1: Financial Controller
- Pain points:
  * Manual data entry errors leading to inaccurate financial reports
  * Inefficient use of time on repetitive tasks, taking away from strategic decision-making
- Goal: Implement an automation tool to streamline financial operations and improve productivity
## Persona 2: Operations Manager
- Pain points:
  * Lack of visibility into business performance due to manual data processing delays
  * Inability to scale operations efficiently due to reliance on manual tasks
- Goal: Adopt an AI-powered automation solution to enhance operational efficiency and drive business growth
