# Marketing Strategy for AI Powered Excel Automation Tool
## Value Proposition
Automate tedious Excel tasks with our AI-powered tool, increasing productivity and efficiency for SMEs.
## Target Audience Summary
Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) seeking to streamline business processes and enhance data-driven decision making.
## Budget Split
* Content Creation: 40%
* Outreach and Advertising: 30%
* Team and Operations: 30%
## Content Plan
* Post 1: Introduction to AI-powered Excel automation (March 20)
* Post 2: Top 3 benefits of using AI in Excel (March 22)
* Post 3: Customer success story - how our tool helped an SME (March 24)
* Reel: Tool demo and walkthrough (March 26)
## Outreach Plan
* Email Campaign: Targeted email to 100 SMEs highlighting the value proposition (March 21)
* DM Campaign: LinkedIn DMs to 50 SME decision-makers introducing our tool (March 25)
