# Persona 1: Financial Controller
## Pain Points:
* Manual data entry and processing in Excel is time-consuming and prone to errors
* Difficulty in scaling business operations due to limited resources and inefficient processes
## Goal:
* To automate financial tasks and improve productivity, enabling the company to make data-driven decisions and drive growth

# Persona 2: Operations Manager
## Pain Points:
* Inefficient business processes and lack of automation lead to wasted time and resources
* Struggling to keep up with the increasing demand for data analysis and insights
## Goal:
* To streamline operations and improve efficiency by leveraging AI-powered Excel automation, enabling the company to focus on strategic growth initiatives
