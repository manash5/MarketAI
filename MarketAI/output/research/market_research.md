# Market Research for AI Powered Excel Automation Tool
## Key Trends:
* Increased adoption of automation tools among SMEs to enhance productivity and efficiency
* Growing demand for AI-powered solutions to streamline business processes
* Rising need for data-driven decision making and analytics
## Competitors:
* Automate Excel (Positioning: User-friendly automation tool for non-technical users)
* Excel Ninja (Positioning: Comprehensive automation platform for power users)
* Sheetgo (Positioning: Cloud-based automation tool for team collaboration)
## Customer Pain Points:
* Manual data entry and processing errors
* Inefficient use of time and resources on repetitive tasks
* Limited ability to scale and analyze large datasets
